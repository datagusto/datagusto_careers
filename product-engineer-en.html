About datagusto

What is datagusto?
A Safety Platform that Proactively Prevents AI Agent Malfunctions

Because AI agents make decisions and act autonomously, while they improve convenience, they also present risks such as unintended actions, information leakage, and irreversible system changes. datagusto develops a proactive intervention safety platform that dynamically generates guardrails based on user intent before the agent executes actions, detecting and controlling hazardous behavior.

View Product Deck

Proactive Intervention Approach

An approach that detects and controls risks before the agent executes, rather than reacting after a problem occurs.

Cross-Stack Safety Layer

Designed to ensure safety across multiple models and environments, without relying on a specific AI model or vendor.

Enterprise-Ready

A trusted architecture that integrates seamlessly with unique client network configurations, authentication flows, and security constraints.

AI agent safety is not a future problem; it is a critical requirement today as enterprise adoption accelerates.
